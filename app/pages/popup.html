To start using CRISP:

  1. Open Developer tools:
    1. Click “Customize and control Google Chrome” (3-dots icon)  at the status bar -> Select “More Tools” -> select “Developer Tools”;
    2. Or press Ctrl+Shift+I;
    3. Or press F12 key.
  2. Open “Elements” tab.
  3. Open "CRISP" sub-tab on the same level with the "Styles" sub-tab.
